The Daily Range Has Statistical Boundaries

Projects sigma-based expansion zones from the daily open, revealing where institutional desks expect rotation, rejection, or exhaustion. This isn't guesswork — it's statistical probability. The indicator tracks consecutive expansion days, asymmetric skew ratios, and historical percentile thresholds to surface high-probability reversals and continuations before they become obvious to the crowd.

Key Features

  • Sigma-based expansion zones (0.5σ to 3σ+) projecting from daily open
  • Dynamic range percentile adjustments based on recent volatility
  • Consecutive expansion day tracking with exhaustion signals
  • Asymmetric skew detection for directional bias
  • 75th and 90th percentile markers (where most ranges terminate)
  • Exhaustion, rejection, and momentum signals with probability scores
  • Volume exhaustion detection at extreme levels
  • Real-time statistics dashboard (skew ratio, win rates, regime)
  • Customizable zone colors and display modes (zones or lines)

When to Use

  • Mean reversion setups at 1.5σ+ extremes with rejection signals
  • Identifying exhaustion after 3+ consecutive expansion days
  • Gauging directional bias through skew ratios (bullish vs bearish asymmetry)
  • Setting profit targets at statistical percentile boundaries
  • Avoiding counter-trend trades when momentum signals fire
  • Confirming compression breakout validity with expansion context
  • Position sizing based on current volatility regime (low/normal/high)

Candle Character Pro

Simple Visual Orderflow Reading

Automatically detects and color-codes key candlestick patterns that signal absorption, liquidity sweeps, and momentum conditions. No manual pattern hunting required — the indicator highlights failed breakouts, inside bar compression, and Judas reversal patterns directly on your chart for faster decision-making and improved entry timing.

Key Features

  • Failed Breakouts: Highlights candles that sweep highs/lows but close back inside previous range (absorption signal)
  • Inside Bars: Marks compression candles fully contained within previous bar's range
  • Judas Failed Breakouts: Identifies dual-sided sweeps that close inside (classic liquidity trap)
  • Judas Valid Breakouts: Detects sweep-then-follow-through patterns (momentum confirmation)
  • Customizable color schemes for each pattern type
  • Adjustable transparency to match your chart aesthetic
  • Real-time pattern detection with no repainting

When to Use

  • Spotting stop hunts and liquidity sweeps before reversals
  • Identifying compression zones that precede expansion moves
  • Confirming absorption at key levels (VWAP, expansion zones, etc.)
  • Distinguishing real breakouts from false breakouts quickly
  • Entry timing refinement using inside bar compression signals
  • Avoiding entries on Judas patterns that signal traps
  • Building confluence with other indicators for higher conviction setups
